Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

We collect your email address when you make a purchase via Gumroad or when you submit the contact form powered by Formspree. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools beyond Vercel's default platform analytics. We collect only what is necessary to deliver your purchase and respond to your enquiries.

How We Use It

Your email address is used to deliver your purchased products and, if you have subscribed to an Edge Pass, to send you update communications related to your packs. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

Third Parties

We use the following third-party services in the operation of OpenWays:

  • Gumroad — payment processing and digital product delivery
  • Formspree — contact form submissions
  • Vercel — website hosting

Data Storage

We do not store payment information. All payment data — including credit card details and billing information — is handled entirely by Gumroad. OpenWays does not have access to your payment details at any point during or after the transaction.

Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hi@openways.net. We will process your request promptly and confirm once your data has been removed from our records.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at hi@openways.net.
