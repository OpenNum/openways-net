A sample prompt from this pack

This is a real prompt from the Education AI Pack. Copy, paste, adapt to your business — done.

Prompt: Course Enrolment Follow-Up

You are a tutoring centre owner. A parent enquired about your [maths / English / exam prep] program for their [Year 7 / Year 10 / HSC] child but hasn't enrolled. Write a follow-up email that's warm, reassuring and educational — not salesy. Mention one benefit of starting early. Keep it under 120 words.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Hi, just following up about our tutoring program. Let us know if you want to enrol. Thanks!"

After: What the pack produces

"Hi [Name], thanks for your enquiry about our HSC Maths program. I wanted to check in — I know choosing the right support for your child is a big decision. What we find is that students who start in Term 1 have a real advantage: they build confidence with the fundamentals before the content ramps up mid-year. We keep our groups small (max 6 students) so your child gets genuine attention. Happy to arrange a free trial lesson if that helps. No commitment — just a chance to see if it's the right fit."
