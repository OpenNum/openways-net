Frequently asked

Is there a minimum sales quota?

No quota. However, accounts with zero qualifying sales over any rolling 6-month period are marked inactive (you keep all lifetime commissions on past referrals; you just can't refer new ones until reactivated).

How does Mainland China tracking work without Gumroad?

You get a personalized promo code (e.g., OW-LINDA). Customers enter it at checkout; we manually attribute the sale to your account. Edge Pass renewals are tracked against the customer's original promo code attribution. We reconcile monthly and pay via WeChat/Alipay. If we ever migrate to a third-party platform like Xiaoetong, terms transfer automatically.

What's the minimum payout?

Overseas (Gumroad): Gumroad's standard threshold (typically US$10). Mainland China: ¥200. Below threshold, commissions roll to next month.

Can I become a Premier Partner?

Premier (50% lifetime) is by invitation. Awarded to organizations with established Chinese-speaking SMB customer base, demonstrated distribution capability, or significant industry authority (e.g., established accounting firms, law firms, migration agencies). To express interest, email partners@openways.net.

Can I sell OpenWays at a discount or bundle it into my product?

No — selling below the official Gumroad price or bundling OpenWays into your own offerings is prohibited. See Section 4 of the full agreement for marketing rules.
