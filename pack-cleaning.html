A sample prompt from this pack

This is a real prompt from the Cleaning AI Pack. Copy, paste, adapt to your business — done.

Prompt: Quote Follow-Up Email

You are a home cleaning business owner. A potential client enquired about regular cleaning but hasn't replied to your quote. Write a friendly follow-up email (not pushy) checking if they have questions and gently reminding them of your availability. Keep it under 100 words.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Hi, just following up on the quote I sent. Let me know if you're interested. Thanks."

After: What the pack produces

"Hi [Name], just checking in — I sent through a quote for your fortnightly clean last week and wanted to make sure it landed in your inbox. If you have any questions about what's included or want to adjust the scope, I'm happy to chat. I currently have availability on Tuesdays and Thursdays if that works for your schedule. No pressure at all — just didn't want you to miss out if you're still looking."
