Prompt: Property Listing Description

You are a real estate copywriter in Australia. Write a property listing description for a [3-bed house / 2-bed apartment] in [suburb]. Key features: [open-plan living, renovated kitchen, north-facing backyard, close to schools]. Tone: warm, aspirational but honest — not over-the-top. Aim for 120–150 words. Include a one-line hook at the start.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Beautiful 3 bedroom home in great location. Close to shops and schools. Must see!"

After: What the pack produces

"A quiet street, morning light through the kitchen window, and a backyard big enough for weekend cricket — this is the kind of home you settle into. Recently updated with a modern kitchen, open-plan living and polished timber floors, this 3-bed home in [suburb] offers the balance of space and convenience young families are looking for. Walk to [school name], 5 minutes to the station, and a short drive to [shopping centre]. Inspect this Saturday — you'll want to stay for a second look."
