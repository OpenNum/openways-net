About the Packs

When you open ChatGPT with no context, you get generic output. These packs give you 20 prompts already written for your specific industry tasks, SOP workflows that make results repeatable, a curated tool stack so you don't waste time researching, and a 7-day plan so you know what to do first. The pack replaces weeks of trial-and-error — not just the typing.
Choose the pack that matches your primary business type. The Beauty pack is for salons, lash studios, beauty therapists and nail artists. Real Estate is for agents and small agencies. Hospitality is for cafes, restaurants and venues. If your business sits between two industries or you're not sure, contact us first — we'll give you a straight recommendation.

Purchasing & Access

All purchases are handled through Gumroad. After checkout, you'll receive an email with a download link giving instant access to all files. You own them permanently — no subscription required to keep accessing them.
Files are delivered as PDFs and/or editable formats depending on the resource type. Guides are typically PDF. Workflows may include editable templates.
Because these are digital products with instant delivery, we generally do not offer refunds after download. That said: we offer a full preview of what's inside before you purchase. We're also happy to answer questions before you buy — including telling you honestly if a pack isn't a good fit for your business. We'd rather you not purchase than purchase the wrong thing. If you genuinely have an issue after buying, reach out and we'll work to resolve it.
Not sure before buying? Contact us first — we'll tell you if it's right for your business.
Each pack is licensed for use within one business. You can share it with staff within your business. Redistribution, resale or sharing outside your business is not permitted.
