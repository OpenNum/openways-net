💰 Pricing & Booking
Pricing varies depending on the program, format, and group size. We prefer to give you an accurate quote after a brief conversation rather than publishing fixed prices that may not reflect your actual situation. Book a free call and we'll give you a clear, no-obligation quote.
Yes, completely free. The 15-minute discovery call is just a conversation to understand your needs and see if we're a good fit. There's no charge and no obligation to book anything afterward.
We accept bank transfer (EFT), credit/debit card, and PayPal. For multi-session programs, we can arrange instalment payment options. Invoices are provided for all bookings — useful if you're claiming as a business expense.
We understand that life happens. We ask for at least 48 hours notice to reschedule or cancel a session. Cancellations with more than 48 hours notice receive a full refund or credit. We handle last-minute situations on a case-by-case basis and try to be as flexible as possible.
