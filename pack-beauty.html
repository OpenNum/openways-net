Prompt: Instagram Caption for Before/After Post

You are a social media copywriter for a beauty salon. Write an Instagram caption for a before-and-after photo of a [lash lift / balayage / gel nails] transformation. The tone should be warm, confident and professional — not salesy. Include a soft call-to-action to book. Keep it under 150 words. End with 5 relevant hashtags.

Before: What you'd usually write

"New lashes done today! 😍 DM me to book. #lashes #beauty"

After: What the pack produces

"That moment when your client sees their lash lift for the first time 🤍 This classic lift adds curl, length and definition — no extensions, no maintenance, just wake-up-and-go lashes that last 6–8 weeks. Ready to try it? Link in bio to book your appointment. #lashlift #naturalbeauty #sydneybeauty #lashgoals #beforeandafter"
