"Won't AI cross the IICRC / ACCI technician boundary?"

"What about Australian Consumer Law & guaranteed-stain-removal claims?"
Real risk — and the pack covers it directly. Three principles: 1) never use "100% stain removal guaranteed" or "bond back guaranteed" wording (ACL prohibits unconditional guarantees); 2) frame outcomes as "best-effort treatment with inspection-ready presentation"; 3) document carpet condition with photos before treatment so disputes have a baseline. Every quote disclaimer in this pack reflects ACL boundaries.

"Will agents accept the bond-back receipt this pack generates?"
Yes — the receipt template uses NSW Tenancy Tribunal-acceptable wording: scope of work performed, treatment method (steam HWE / dry low-moisture), drying-time disclosure, post-service inspection note, and ABN / public liability insurance reference. Tenants can hand it directly to property managers. The pack also includes a re-treatment policy template if a first inspection is rejected.

This pack handles everything around the job — bilingual customer enquiries, steam-vs-dry quoting, treatment-plan explanations, end-of-lease receipts, recurring proposals, Google Reviews, and Xiaohongshu before-and-after content.
