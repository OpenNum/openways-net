A sample prompt from this pack

This is a real prompt from the TCM AI Pack. Copy, paste, adapt to your practice — done.

Prompt: Patient Education Post (Social Media)

You are a TCM practitioner writing a social media post to educate potential patients. Topic: [how acupuncture helps with insomnia / the role of herbal medicine in digestion / what to expect at your first TCM appointment]. Tone: warm, knowledgeable, approachable — not clinical. Keep it under 120 words. End with a soft call-to-action to book a consultation.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Acupuncture can help with many conditions. Book an appointment today! #tcm #acupuncture"

After: What the pack produces

"Can't sleep? You're not alone — and it's one of the most common reasons patients walk through our door. In TCM, insomnia isn't just about the mind. It's often connected to heart, liver or spleen imbalance. Acupuncture works by calming the nervous system and restoring your body's natural sleep rhythm — without medication. Most patients notice a difference within 3–4 sessions. If you've been lying awake wondering whether TCM could help, the answer is probably yes. Book a consultation and let's talk about what's going on."
