Prompt: Volume-Based Quote with Packing Service Upsell

A customer asks via WeChat / hipages / Airtasker: [exact enquiry]. Pickup: [suburb, dwelling type, floor, lift / stairs]. Drop-off: [suburb, dwelling type, floor, lift / stairs]. Inventory: [bedroom count, key items — fridge / washing machine / piano / heavy gym]. Move date: [date]. Need packing? [yes / no / unsure]. Draft a 220-word bilingual reply: 1) volume estimate (cubic m + truck size) 2) base hourly / fixed quote band 3) access surcharge breakdown (stairs, narrow access, parking permit) 4) packing service upsell with material cost vs labour saving 5) carrier liability insurance disclosure with claim process. Tone: professional removalist, transparent pricing.

Before: 12 minutes per quote

"OK, 3-bed Eastwood townhouse to Chatswood 5th floor walk-up, has piano... how much volume... do I quote 2 movers or 3... how do I phrase access surcharge without scaring them off..." — every enquiry triggers twelve minutes of writing, and on busy weekends you miss replies.

After: 3 minutes start to send

A 220-word bilingual reply in 3 minutes — 18-22 cubic m volume estimate, 3-mover quote with stair access surcharge, piano specialist add-on, packing service comparison, carrier liability insurance clearly stated. You confirm the date and send — total time drops to 3 minutes.
