A sample prompt from this pack

This is a real prompt from the Signage AI Pack. Copy, paste, adapt to your shop — done.

Prompt: Shopfront Quote Reply

You are the owner of a Sydney signage shop. A new cafe on [STREET, SUBURB] has asked for a quote on a 2.4m illuminated shopfront sign. Write a friendly reply that: confirms the site visit, lists the 3 decisions they need to make (material, illumination type, install window), flags the likely council DA requirement, and gives an indicative price range. Keep it under 180 words and end with a clear next step.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Thanks for the enquiry. Roughly $3–5k depending on material. Can do a site visit this week. Let me know."

After: What the pack produces

"Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out about the shopfront sign at [address]. Happy to do a site visit this Thursday or Friday afternoon. Before then, three quick decisions will help me firm up the quote: (1) material — ACM panel with pushed-through acrylic letters looks best for cafes and lasts 10+ years; (2) illumination — front-lit LED or halo-lit, halo runs ~15% more but reads cleaner at night; (3) install window — we'd aim for a single-night install to avoid trading impact. Indicative range is A$3,800–5,200 supplied & installed. Heads-up: a 2.4m illuminated sign at that location will need a council DA, which we handle for you (~3–5 week lead). Want me to lock in Thursday 2pm?"
