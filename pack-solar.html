A sample prompt from this pack

This is a real prompt from the Solar AI Pack. Copy, paste, adapt to your business — done.

Prompt: Solar Proposal Cover Letter

You are a solar installation company owner. Write a cover letter to accompany a residential solar proposal for a [3-bed house / 4-bed house] in [suburb]. System size: [6.6kW]. Estimated annual savings: [$1,800]. Tone: professional, educational, not pushy. Explain the value simply. Keep it under 150 words. End with a clear next step.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Please find attached our quote for a 6.6kW solar system. Let us know if you have any questions."

After: What the pack produces

"Hi [Name], thanks for the opportunity to quote on your solar installation. I've put together a system designed specifically for your roof and energy usage — a 6.6kW system with [panel brand] panels and a [inverter brand] inverter, both backed by industry-leading warranties. Based on your current electricity bills, this system should save you around $1,800 per year — meaning it effectively pays for itself within [X] years. I've attached the full proposal with system specs, savings breakdown and warranty details. The next step is a quick site inspection (takes about 20 minutes) to confirm everything. Happy to book that in whenever suits you."
