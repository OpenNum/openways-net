About

We build AI toolkits for small businesses

OpenWays exists to close the gap between "AI sounds useful" and "AI is actually working in my business." We do that through practical, industry-specific resources — not courses, not coaching, not generic guides.

What we do

Small business owners are being told to "just use AI" — but without any practical guidance on how to do that in the context of their specific business. Most resources are either too generic, too technical, or built for enterprise teams with IT support.

OpenWays packs are different. Each one is built around a single industry — its actual workflows, tasks, and pain points. The prompts in the Beauty AI Pack are written for beauty businesses. The SOP Workflows reflect how a salon actually operates. The 7-Day Action Plan is paced for a business owner running everything themselves.

Our goal is simple: a business owner should be able to buy a pack on Monday and have AI genuinely integrated into their daily work by the following Monday. Not just opened. Not just explored. Actually working.

How we think

What we believe

Practical over theoretical

Every resource is written around what you'll actually do — not what's theoretically possible with AI.

Industry-specific over generic

Generic AI advice is everywhere. Industry-specific, tested resources are rare. That's the gap we fill.

Products over services

We build structured resources you can use independently — not ongoing engagements you need us for.

Clarity over complexity

Plain language, step-by-step structure. No jargon, no unnecessary technical depth.

Honest about limitations

AI is powerful but not magic. We focus on what it actually does well for small businesses — not hype.

Staying current

The AI landscape changes fast. We keep our packs current through an ongoing update process.
