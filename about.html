About OpenWays

Built by someone who watched small businesses struggle with AI

Not a training program. Not a consultancy. A structured toolkit that gives small business owners a practical system for using AI — built around how their specific industry actually works.

Who's behind OpenWays

OpenWays was built out of a specific frustration: watching small business owners — especially in service industries — spend real money and real time on AI courses, workshops and generic guides, then still not having anything actually running in their business.

The problem wasn't knowledge. It was the gap between "I understand that AI is useful" and "AI is doing something useful in my business right now." That gap is a systems problem, not an education problem. These packs are designed to close it.

Our Process

How each pack is built

Every pack follows the same four-step build process. This is why the resources feel like they were made for your business — because they were.

01

Map real weekly business tasks

Before writing a single prompt, we map out what a business in that industry actually does every week — the messages they send, the content they need, the admin that takes time. The pack is built around those tasks, not around what AI can theoretically do.

02

Choose the smallest useful AI stack

We identify the minimum set of AI tools that covers those tasks — prioritising tools with free tiers, avoiding unnecessary subscriptions. The goal is to get a business owner operational with as little friction as possible.

03

Build prompts and workflows around actual use cases

Every prompt is written for a specific industry task — not adapted from a generic template. The SOP workflows are built around how the business actually operates, so the AI output slots into existing routines rather than creating new ones.

04

Package into a one-week implementation path

All resources are sequenced into a 7-day plan so the business owner always knows what to do next. By the end of the first week, AI is running in the business — not sitting in a downloads folder.

Why OpenWays exists

There is no shortage of AI content. Courses, YouTube channels, newsletters, workshops — all telling small business owners to "start using AI." Almost none of them explain how to use it in the context of a specific business, with specific tasks, running on a specific day of the week.

The result is that most business owners get stuck between "I know AI is useful" and "I actually use AI every day." They watch demos. They try things. They don't have a system. And eventually the tab closes and nothing changes.

OpenWays packs are designed to close that gap — not by teaching AI, but by giving business owners a structured, ready-to-use system they can implement in a single week.

The Method

Why this works when generic AI advice doesn't

01

Industry-specific, not adapted

Each pack is built from scratch for one business type. The prompts in the Beauty pack are written for beauty businesses. The workflows reflect how a salon actually operates — not how a hypothetical "small business" might. Generic AI advice fails because it doesn't account for the specifics of your work. Industry-specific tools work because they do.

02

Structured, not open-ended

A blank prompt box is intimidating. A structured system isn't. Each pack removes the blank page problem by giving you specific prompts for specific situations, workflows for specific tasks, and a day-by-day plan for your first week. You don't need to figure out how to use AI — the pack already figured that out for your industry.

03

Implementation, not education

Understanding AI and using AI are different things. You don't need to understand how a large language model works to use it effectively in your business. The packs skip the theory entirely. They start at "here's what to set up" and end at "here's what you do on Tuesday morning." The goal is AI running in your operations — not AI knowledge sitting in your memory.

04

Systems, not habits

Individual AI use fades. Systems persist. The SOP Workflows in each pack are designed to make your best AI output repeatable — so you're not starting from scratch every time you want to write a caption or reply to a client. You build once, use indefinitely.

05

Updated, not frozen

AI tools change fast. A prompt that works perfectly today might need adjusting in six months. An Update Pass keeps your pack current — with revised recommendations and new resources as the landscape evolves. Your toolkit stays useful without you having to track the AI industry yourself.

What OpenWays is not

Not hype
