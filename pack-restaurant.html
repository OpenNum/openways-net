Prompt: Google Review Response (Positive)

You are the owner of a [cafe / restaurant / takeaway]. A customer left this 5-star Google review: "[paste review]". Write a warm, professional reply that thanks them, mentions something specific from their review, and gently invites them back. Keep it under 80 words. Don't use exclamation marks excessively.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Thanks for the great review! We appreciate it 😊"

After: What the pack produces

"Thank you so much for your kind words about our barramundi — it's one of our chef's favourites too. We're glad the outdoor seating hit the spot on such a beautiful evening. Hope to see you back soon — maybe next time you can try the sticky date pudding. It pairs perfectly with a flat white."
