Frequently asked

Which industries do you cover?

All 24 OpenWays packs (beauty, restaurant, real estate, accounting, clinic, education, renovation, solar, cleaning, signage, immigration, lawyer, insurance, logistics, mortgage — plus international editions). Industries outside this list are still welcome — Vince will assess fit during the quote stage.

What's the typical price range?

Quick Setup starts at A$299. Founder Hour at A$599. Enterprise is quoted per engagement, typically A$2,000-8,000 depending on scope. Final price is confirmed within 24 hours of your application — no hidden fees, no upsells.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes for Founder Hour and Enterprise tiers — mutual NDA included by default. Quick Setup is informal; if you need an NDA at that tier, just mention it in the form.

Is remote (Zoom) the same price as in-person?

Yes — same pricing globally. In-person is available within Sydney metro at no extra cost. Travel beyond Sydney is quoted separately.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Quick Setup: full refund within 7 days if you find no value. Founder Hour: 50% deposit refundable up to 48 hours before kickoff; final balance only charged on delivery. Enterprise: terms agreed in scope letter before deposit.
