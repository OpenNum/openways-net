Update Pass

Your toolkit should evolve as AI does

The AI tools you use today will change. Prompts that work brilliantly now may need adjusting in six months. Tool pricing shifts. Better options emerge. The Update Pass keeps your pack current — without you having to track any of it yourself.

Why updates matter more in AI than anywhere else

In most industries, a guide written two years ago is still mostly useful. In AI, two years is a generation. The tools in your pack today may change their pricing, add features, merge with competitors, or simply be replaced by something significantly better.

More importantly: the prompts that get you great results from today's AI models won't always produce the same results as those models improve. Prompting approaches that felt necessary in early ChatGPT are now unnecessary. New capabilities mean new shortcuts. What was a two-step workflow becomes one step.

The Update Pass means you benefit from those improvements automatically — without having to follow AI news, test new models yourself, or figure out what changed.

What You Receive

What an update actually contains

Updates are released when something meaningful changes — not on a schedule. Here's what they cover.

✍️

Revised Prompt Library

As AI models improve, better ways to phrase prompts emerge. Updated prompts are tested and rewritten so your results stay sharp — not stuck at the quality level of when you first purchased.

🔧

Updated Tool Stack

When a recommended tool changes pricing, adds a meaningful new feature, or gets outperformed by something better — your Tool Stack gets revised to reflect the current best options for your industry.

🔁

Revised SOP Workflows

New automation paths emerge. Tools that previously required three steps can now do one. When meaningful efficiency gains become available, your workflows get updated to reflect them.

📰

Industry Notes

Curated notes on what's changing in AI that specifically matters for your business type. Not a general AI newsletter — specific observations relevant to beauty, real estate, hospitality, or whichever industry your pack covers.

📦

Annual Add-ons

Occasional additional resources added to the pack over the year — new prompt categories, extended workflow templates, or supplementary guides as new use cases become practical for small businesses.
