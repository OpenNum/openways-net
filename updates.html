Update Pass

You run your business. We keep your AI sharp.

AI tools change every week — new models, new pricing, new features. We track all of it, filter what's relevant for your industry, and update your toolkit automatically. You don't have to read AI news, test new tools, or figure out what changed. Just run your business.

The Problem

Big companies have AI teams. Now you do too.

AI changes every week. New tools launch. Existing tools change their pricing. Models get smarter — and the prompts that worked last month may need rewriting. Features that required workarounds become one-click solutions.

Large companies have CTOs, innovation teams, and dedicated AI departments tracking all of this. They have people whose full-time job is to evaluate new tools, test new models, and update internal systems.

As a small business owner, you don't have that time. You're running operations, serving customers, and managing a team. You shouldn't have to spend your evenings reading AI newsletters and wondering if your tools are still the right ones.

The OpenWays Update Pass is your outsourced AI department. We do the monitoring, filtering, testing, and updating — and deliver the results directly to you. Your prompts stay sharp. Your tool stack stays current. Your workflows stay efficient.

You just keep running your business.

Your business already has a bookkeeper. Now it has an AI team. For less than a coffee a month.

What You Receive

What an update actually contains

Updates are released when something meaningful changes — not on a schedule. Here's what they cover.

✍️

Revised Prompt Library

As AI models improve, better ways to phrase prompts emerge. Updated prompts are tested and rewritten so your results stay sharp — not stuck at the quality level of when you first purchased.

🔧

Updated Tool Stack

When a recommended tool changes pricing, adds a meaningful new feature, or gets outperformed by something better — your Tool Stack gets revised to reflect the current best options for your industry.

🔁

Revised SOP Workflows

New automation paths emerge. Tools that previously required three steps can now do one. When meaningful efficiency gains become available, your workflows get updated to reflect them.

📊

Industry AI Brief

A quarterly brief on AI developments specific to your industry — new tools gaining traction, emerging use cases, what competitors are doing with AI, and trends worth watching.

📡

AI Market Digest

What changed in the AI landscape that affects your business. Which tools changed pricing. Which new features matter. Which hype to ignore. We do the filtering so you don't have to.

🎯

Priority Access

Early access to new industry packs, templates, and events before they go public. Update Pass holders hear about it first.

Our Process

How we build updates

🔍
Continuous Monitoring

We continuously monitor AI tool market changes — pricing shifts, new features, new entrants, and tools that shut down or pivot.

🧪
Prompt Testing

When new models launch, we test every prompt for quality shifts. If a prompt needs rewriting to maintain or improve output quality, we rewrite it.

💰
Pricing Re-evaluation

When tool pricing changes, we re-evaluate our recommendations. If a free tool moves to paid, or a better free alternative appears, we update the stack.

📈
Industry Trend Tracking

Industry trends tracked through public data and industry sources — so your brief reflects what's actually happening in your space, not generic AI hype.
