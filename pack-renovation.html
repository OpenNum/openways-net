Prompt: Project Progress Update Email

You are a home renovation contractor. Write a weekly progress update email to a client whose [kitchen / bathroom / extension] renovation is in week [3]. Cover: what was completed so far, what's planned next week, and any decisions the client needs to make. Tone: professional, reassuring, concise. Keep it under 150 words.

Before: What you'd usually write

"Hi, work is going well. Tiling starts next week. Let me know if you have questions."

After: What the pack produces

"Hi [Name], quick update on your kitchen renovation — we had a great week. The new cabinetry is fully installed, benchtops have been templated (final measurement for your stone tops), and the electrician completed the rough-in for your pendant lights. Next week: tiling begins on Monday, and plumbing for the sink and dishwasher is booked for Wednesday. One decision needed from you: the splashback tile — we'll need your final choice by Thursday to stay on schedule. I've attached two options from the range we discussed. Happy to jump on a quick call if you'd like to talk it through."

How to use AI tools in your home renovation business

A practical guide for Australian renovation contractors and builders

Renovation businesses win work through reputation, referrals, and online presence. But building that presence — social media content, Google reviews, quote follow-ups, before-and-after posts — takes consistent time that most renovation operators can't find in their working day.

AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude are now used by renovation businesses across Australia to produce this content efficiently — in the evening or on weekends, without hours of writing from scratch.

  1. Project showcase content. Turn your job photos and descriptions into compelling before-and-after posts for Instagram, Facebook, and Google — the content that generates new quote requests.
  2. Quote follow-up sequences. AI drafts professional follow-up messages for clients who've received a quote but haven't confirmed — converting more of your existing leads without starting from scratch.
  3. Client testimonial posts. Turn a great review or client comment into a professional social post — with variables for the job type, suburb, and what you delivered.
  4. Google Business Profile content. Regular posts about completed projects, service areas, and renovation tips — the consistent activity that improves local search ranking.
  5. Inquiry and review responses. Reply to every inquiry and Google review professionally — the consistent communication that builds trust before a client has even met you.

The Renovation AI Pack gives you the exact prompts and workflows to build your renovation business's online reputation and convert more quote inquiries into booked jobs.

How we built this pack: We mapped the real weekly workflows of renovation contractors and home improvement businesses — from project showcase posts to quote follow-ups to before-and-after content creation. Every prompt was tested across multiple AI models. Every tool was evaluated for practical trade industry use.
