## 样本综合判断

### 住宅 pest control 最常见服务组合
- general pest treatment
- cockroach / ant / spider control
- rodent control
- termite inspection / treatment
- end of lease flea treatment
- wasp / possum / bird 等专项

### weed control 最常见服务组合
- lawn weed spraying
- hand weeding + herbicide treatment
- pre-emergent / post-emergent
- seasonal weed management
- fertiliser / turf recovery 搭配销售

- licensed and insured
- local your city technicians
- family / pet friendly options

- guarantee / re-service period

## 结论
这个行业非常适合做“总入口 + 子服务页”结构，但最好不要把“杀虫”和“除草”写得完全混在一起。更自然的做法是：
- 首页讲 property protection / healthy home & outdoor spaces
- 一级导航拆成 Pest Control、Termite、Commercial、Weed Control
- 再按具体需求开子页
