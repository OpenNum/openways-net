## 4. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕洗不干净，老污渍还在
可用话术：
- Deep carpet cleaning for traffic marks, spills and everyday build-up.
- Targeted stain treatment based on the stain type and carpet condition.
- We’ll inspect the carpet first and recommend the most suitable cleaning method.

### 痛点 2：怕报价不透明，现场加价
可用话术：
- Clear, upfront pricing before the job starts.
- Room-based quotes with optional extras explained in advance.
- No confusing add-ons, just a clear scope and honest recommendations.

### 痛点 3：怕太久不干，影响搬家或使用
可用话术：
- We explain expected drying times before we begin.
- Fast-drying guidance to help you get back to normal sooner.
- Ask us whether steam cleaning or a lower-moisture option is better for your carpet.

### 痛点 4：怕退租验房不过
可用话术：
- Carpet steam cleaning for move-out and end-of-lease preparation.
- Freshen high-use areas before inspection day.
- Ideal for tenants who need a professional clean before handing the keys back.

### 痛点 5：家里有宠物或孩子，担心异味和刺激
可用话术：
- Suitable cleaning approaches for family homes and pet households.
- Help reduce odours and refresh carpeted areas affected by everyday pet life.
- We’ll advise on aftercare and drying so the space is comfortable again as soon as possible.

## 9. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 绝对化承诺“所有污渍 100% 去除”
- 没看现场就承诺“保证完全无味”
- 把 steam cleaning、dry cleaning、shampooing 混为一谈
- 只写低价，不解释包含范围
- 用过度刺激型词汇暗示药剂无风险但不给边界说明

## 10. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果做 5 个页面参考文件，文案最好保持：
- 一页一个主场景
- 每页把 cleaning method、适合谁、包含什么、预计干燥和预约方式讲清楚
- 退租页要更强调 timing、receipt、inspection 语境
- 宠物页要更强调异味、局部污渍、家庭友好感
- 如果面向华人市场，中文表达要更生活化，少堆专业术语
