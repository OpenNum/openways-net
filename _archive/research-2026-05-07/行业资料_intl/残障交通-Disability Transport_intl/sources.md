## 已直接参考的公开网页
1. Transport for London, Dial-a-Ride
   - https://tfl.gov.uk/modes/dial-a-ride/
   - 关键信号：membership、booking、web portal、app、运营时段、取消与用户教育

2. Roundtrip Health, homepage
   - https://roundtriphealth.com/
   - 关键信号：降低 no-show、整合多类车辆、节省 care coordinator 时间、数据洞察

3. Roundtrip Health, customer story: Meet Ivone Melendez
   - https://roundtriphealth.com/blog/meet-ivone-melendez/
   - 关键信号：传统 cab 协调流程非常耗时，线上调度显著减少人工沟通与传真流程

4. RouteGenie, NEMT software
   - https://routegenie.com/
   - 关键信号：行业核心模块集中在 scheduling、routing、dispatch、billing、fleet、driver app、client communication

5. Uber Health
   - https://www.uberhealth.com/au/en/
   - 关键信号：减少 no-show、优化 discharge workflow、提高资金使用透明度、注重 safety/privacy

6. Lyft Healthcare
   - https://www.lyft.com/healthcare
   - 关键信号：health systems、NEMT brokers、payers 采用；door-to-door light assistance；无智能手机用户也可使用；no-show 与 call center 成本改善
