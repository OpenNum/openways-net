## 4. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：不知道选哪种材料
可用话术：
- Not sure whether carpet, hybrid, timber or vinyl is right for your space? We’ll guide you based on budget, lifestyle and room use.
- We help you compare flooring options by durability, water resistance, comfort and overall look.
- Get practical advice, not just a product catalogue.

### 痛点 2：怕报价不透明，后面一直加项
可用话术：
- Clear quotes with product, installation and key extras explained upfront.
- We’ll talk you through underlay, trims, floor prep and removal so there are fewer surprises later.
- Honest recommendations for what your project really needs.

### 痛点 3：怕施工做得粗，细节不好看
可用话术：
- Neat installation with attention to edges, stairs, doorways and finishing details.
- Accurate measuring and experienced installers for a cleaner final result.
- We focus on the details that make a new floor feel properly finished.

### 痛点 4：怕预算有限，效果却一般
可用话术：
- Flooring packages for different budgets, from practical rental updates to premium home finishes.
- We’ll help you get the best value without overspending on the wrong product.
- Cost-effective options that still look polished and wear well.

### 痛点 5：怕影响入住、出租或营业
可用话术：
- We schedule around your move, renovation or business operations where possible.
- Fast quoting and organised installation to help keep your project moving.
- Ideal for homes, rentals and commercial spaces needing reliable turnaround.

## 9. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 绝对化承诺“最便宜”“全您所在的城市最低价”
- 没看现场就承诺完全没有额外项目
- 把 waterproof、water-resistant、scratch-proof 混成一回事
- 只讲材料，不讲安装和基层条件
- 对施工周期、旧地面情况、门缝和收边不给边界说明

## 10. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果做页面或社媒内容，建议保持：
- 一页一个主场景或主材质
- 每页都讲清楚适合谁、预算层级、施工包含什么、是否有 showroom / free quote
- 中文页可更重实用和成交，英文页可更重专业和审美
- 多用 before / after、样板、材料对比、翻新案例，而不是空泛口号
