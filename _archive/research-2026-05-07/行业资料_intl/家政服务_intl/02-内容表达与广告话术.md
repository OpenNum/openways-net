## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕不靠谱
可用话术：
- Fully insured, police-checked cleaners you can trust in your home.
- Trusted by your city families for reliable, consistent cleaning.
- The same cleaner each visit whenever possible.

### 痛点 2：怕麻烦，怕预订慢
可用话术：
- Get an instant quote and book online in minutes.
- Fast, simple booking with clear upfront pricing.
- Same-day and next-day bookings available in selected your city suburbs.

### 痛点 3：怕只做表面
可用话术：
- Detailed cleaning that covers kitchens, bathrooms, floors, surfaces and overlooked areas.
- From regular upkeep to deep cleans, we tailor the service to your home.
- Add oven, window, rangehood and carpet cleaning when you need extra detail.

### 痛点 4：怕退租不过
可用话术：
- End of lease cleaning designed to help you leave the property inspection-ready.
- Thorough move-out cleaning with optional carpet, oven and window add-ons.
- A detailed checklist to help reduce last-minute issues with your agent or property manager.

### 痛点 5：怕长期服务不稳定
可用话术：
- Weekly and fortnightly cleaning for a consistently clean home.
- Regular services with flexible scheduling and no lock-in contracts.
- Designed for busy households who want less stress and more time back.

## 8. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 过度承诺“100%拿回 bond”
- 太像廉价低质外包的“lowest price guaranteed”堆砌
- 没有依据就写“number 1 in your city”
- 把清洁、保姆、护理、养老支持混成一个模糊大词

## 9. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果要做 5 个页面参考文件，文案最好保持：
- 一页一个主场景
- 每页都把“适合谁、包含什么、为什么选你、怎么预约”讲清楚
- 把 end of lease 与 regular clean 分开写
- NDIS / aged care 用词要更稳，不要用普通促销语硬卖
