## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：不知道问题是清洁还是设备
可用话术：
- We do more than skim leaves. We check water balance, circulation and equipment performance so the real issue gets addressed.
- Not sure if it’s a chemical issue or equipment fault? We inspect both before recommending the next step.

### 痛点 2：怕水刚清又变绿
可用话术：
- We focus on the cause, not just the appearance, so your pool stays clearer for longer.
- Green and cloudy pools often need more than a quick clean. We test, treat, clean and monitor recovery properly.

### 痛点 3：怕报价不清楚
可用话术：
- Clear quotes with labour, chemicals and parts explained separately.
- We’ll outline what’s included in the visit, what needs follow-up, and what can wait.

### 痛点 4：怕夏天用的时候出问题
可用话术：
- Get your pool swim-ready before the heat, the holidays and the next busy weekend.
- Pre-summer servicing helps reduce avoidable breakdowns when you need the pool most.

### 痛点 5：怕长期维护不稳定
可用话术：
- Regular weekly or fortnightly pool service to keep water balanced, equipment checked and your pool ready to use.
- Scheduled maintenance helps catch small issues before they turn into expensive repairs.

## 8. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 过度承诺“one visit guaranteed to fix every green pool”
- 没看现场就写死所有价格
- 只强调 cheapest，不强调 diagnosis 和 safety
- 把 pool cleaning 和 pool renovation 混成一个模糊品类

## 9. 对后续内容生成的提醒
- 每页尽量聚焦一个主场景，不要全部堆在首页
- 化学平衡要写得让普通业主听得懂
- 设备问题页要强调 inspection / diagnosis / parts not always included
- 夏季内容要更偏 ready-to-use，冬季内容要更偏 prevent and protect
