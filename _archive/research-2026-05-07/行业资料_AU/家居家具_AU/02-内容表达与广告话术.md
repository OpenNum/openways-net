## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕买回去尺寸不合适
可用话术：
- Find the right fit for your home with size options designed for apartments and family homes.
- Need a better fit? We offer custom sizing on selected pieces.
- Visit our Sydney showroom and compare dimensions, finishes and comfort in person.

### 痛点 2：怕图片好看，实物一般
可用话术：
- See, touch and experience the materials in our Sydney showroom.
- Quality you can feel, from the frame to the fabric.
- Crafted with durable materials chosen for everyday living.

### 痛点 3：怕风格不搭，买散了
可用话术：
- Build a home that feels cohesive with furniture collections designed to work together.
- From sofas to dining and bedroom pieces, create a consistent look across your home.
- Need help pulling it together? Ask about our in-store styling guidance.

### 痛点 4：怕交期不清楚，送货麻烦
可用话术：
- Clear lead time guidance before you order.
- Delivery options designed to make furnishing your home easier.
- White glove delivery and assembly available on selected orders.

### 痛点 5：怕不耐用，不适合家庭使用
可用话术：
- Built for everyday comfort and made to last.
- Family-friendly materials and practical finishes for real homes.
- Timeless furniture designed to live with, not just look at.

### 痛点 6：定制类怕沟通不清楚
可用话术：
- Choose the size, fabric and finish that suits your space.
- Made-to-order furniture with clear selections and lead times.
- Work with our team to create a piece tailored to your home and lifestyle.

## 8. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 没依据就写“lowest prices in Sydney”
- 过度承诺“luxury”但页面和产品图撑不住
- 把所有风格都塞在一个品牌里，导致调性混乱
- 定制类不写 lead time 和流程，只讲好看
- 家具站过度像装修站，忽略产品细节、尺寸和材质信息

## 9. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果要做 5 个页面参考文件，文案最好保持：
- 一页一个主类目或主场景
- 每页都把“适合谁、有哪些款、可否定制、如何下单 / 到店”讲清楚
- 把 showroom、delivery、lead time、materials 这些用户真正在意的问题讲透
- 如果面向华人客户，可补“中文服务 + 尺寸建议 + 户型适配”这类转化细节
