## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕不专业，像临时家政
可用话术：
- Commercial cleaning built around your site, schedule and hygiene requirements.
- Professional office and strata cleaning with clear scopes, trained staff and reliable follow-through.
- A cleaner workspace without disrupting your team, tenants or customers.

### 痛点 2：怕沟通麻烦，报价不清
可用话术：
- Get a clear quote based on your site, service scope and preferred frequency.
- We confirm what’s included before the clean, so there are fewer surprises on the day.
- Chinese communication available, with English quotes and invoices if needed.

### 痛点 3：怕长期合作不稳定
可用话术：
- Daily, weekly and fortnightly cleaning plans tailored to your site.
- Consistent service with a clear checklist and a responsive point of contact.
- Ideal for offices, retail spaces and strata properties that need dependable ongoing care.

### 痛点 4：怕退租验房返工
可用话术：
- Detailed end of lease cleaning designed to help you leave the property inspection-ready.
- Add carpet steam cleaning, oven cleaning and window cleaning in one move-out booking.
- A practical clean for tenants who want fewer last-minute issues at final inspection.

### 痛点 5：怕公区一直被投诉
可用话术：
- Keep lobbies, lifts, corridors and bin rooms clean, presentable and easier to manage.
- Regular common area cleaning that supports a better experience for residents and visitors.
- Strata cleaning built for shared spaces, high-touch areas and recurring maintenance.

## 9. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 绝对承诺“100% bond back”
- 没根据就写“Sydney No.1”
- 把 office cleaning 写成家庭保洁风
- 首页什么都写，导致商业清洁和退租清洁混在一起
- 用太多廉价词，削弱 B2B 信任感

## 10. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果要做 5 个页面参考文件，建议保持：
- 每页只讲一个主场景
- Hero 里直接写适合谁、做什么、怎么预约
- commercial / office / strata / end of lease 分开写
- carpet 和 window 作为高频加购，在 FAQ、套餐、对比表里反复出现
- 中文市场内容里重点突出“沟通效率 + 本地规范感”，而不是只强调便宜
