# 商业清洁 / 退租清洁本地样本（悉尼 / 澳大利亚）

更新时间：2026-04-10

## 样本选择说明
以下样本主要用于观察：
- 服务切分方式
- 本地化表达
- B2B 与 C 端页面差异
- 加购项如何组合
- 后续适合仿写的角度

## 样本 1. Airtasker Sydney Office Cleaning
- 链接：https://www.airtasker.com/au/services/office-cleaning/sydney/
- 主打：office cleaning task 型需求撮合
- 关键表达：
  - first thing in the morning or at the end of the workday
  - regular office clean anywhere from daily to weekly
  - remove rubbish / clean floors / kitchen and bathrooms / wipe desks, windows and other surfaces
- 页面特点：
  - 很直白地写出办公清洁应该避开上班时间
  - 把办公室清洁拆成垃圾、地面、茶水间、洗手间、桌面和玻璃
- 可借鉴点：
  - 非常适合做 office landing page 的 service checklist
  - 可以把“营业前后可安排”作为核心卖点

## 样本 2. Airtasker Sydney Commercial Cleaning
- 链接：https://www.airtasker.com/au/services/commercial-cleaning/sydney/
- 主打：commercial cleaning 总入口
- 关键表达：
  - commercial cleaners usually work after business hours
  - regular cleaning on a daily or weekly basis
  - kitchens and bathrooms / floor cleaning / carpet cleaning
- 页面特点：
  - 比 office cleaning 更宽，明确带出 retail、event 后清洁、地面维护、地毯深洗
  - 把 commercial cleaning 做成总入口，再往下分细项
- 可借鉴点：
  - 非常适合首页或总服务页框架
  - 适合作为“office、retail、clinic、warehouse amenities”总导航页

## 样本 3. Airtasker Sydney End of Lease Cleaning
- 链接：https://www.airtasker.com/au/services/end-of-lease-cleaning/sydney/
- 主打：退租清洁任务说明
- 关键表达：
  - final inspection
  - wall and floor cleaning
  - oven cleaning
  - window and blind cleaning
  - additional services like carpet cleaning and pest control
- 页面特点：
  - 写得非常像用户任务清单
  - 强调“搬空后更适合做彻底清洁”
  - 自然带出 carpet、window、garden 等追加项
- 可借鉴点：
  - 很适合做退租 checklist 区块
  - 可把“inspection-ready”写成核心结果词

## 样本 4. Airtasker Sydney Carpet Cleaning
- 链接：https://www.airtasker.com/au/services/carpet-cleaning/sydney/
- 主打：carpet steam / dry clean / shampoo
- 关键表达：
  - steam cleaning, dry cleaning, shampooing
  - stain removal
  - rug cleaning
  - couch and upholstery cleaning
  - mattress cleaning
- 页面特点：
  - 非常清晰地说明 carpet 其实可以向软装深洗延展
  - 强化“去渍、除味、过敏原”逻辑
- 可借鉴点：
  - 很适合作为退租或商业清洁的 add-on 页面参考
  - 可用于提高客单价，不只卖 carpet 本身

## 样本 5. Airtasker Sydney Window Cleaning
- 链接：https://www.airtasker.com/au/services/window-cleaning/sydney/
- 主打：window / glass surface cleaning
- 关键表达：
  - inside and outside windows
  - frames and tracks

  - mirror cleaning
- 页面特点：
  - 不只写玻璃，还写 tracks、sills、screens、balcony glass

## 样本 6. Clean Group / commercialcleaning.au
- 链接：https://commercialcleaning.au/
- 主打：Sydney commercial cleaning / office cleaning 总站
- 关键表达：
  - no lock-in contracts
  - 25+ years of experience
  - eco friendly cleaning
  - single account manager
  - police checks / triple ISO / liability cover
- 页面特点：
  - 很典型的 B2B 站点写法
  - 强调 coverage、compliance、monthly walkthrough、scope adjustments
  - 明显不是“家政型清洁”表达，而是 facility / operations 视角
- 可借鉴点：
  - 很适合做商业清洁首页与报价页框架
  - 适合参考“合规与流程感”的表述

## 样本 7. Clean Group Strata Cleaning Sydney
- 链接：https://commercialcleaning.au/strata-cleaning-sydney/
- 主打：strata / common area cleaning
- 关键表达：
  - common area maintenance

## 样本综合判断

### 最常见主赛道
- office cleaning
- commercial cleaning
- end of lease cleaning
- strata / common area cleaning

### 最常见加购项
- carpet steam cleaning
- interior / accessible exterior window cleaning
- oven cleaning
- upholstery / chairs
- rubbish removal
- pressure cleaning（部分场景）

### 最常见信任元素
- insured
- police checked
- trained staff
- years of experience
- ISO / compliance / safety
- eco-friendly products

### 最常见转化动作
- get a free quote
- request site inspection
- book online
- call now
- ask for a customised cleaning plan

### 最常见页面框架
1. 城市 + 服务名 Hero
2. 信任 / 合规条
3. 服务场景与适合对象
4. checklist / inclusions
5. add-ons
6. FAQ / process / quote CTA

## 对后续仿写最有价值的观察

### 商业客户页面
要更像：
- operations support
- hygiene and presentation
- reliable roster
- site-specific scope
- after-hours service

### 退租客户页面
要更像：
- moving support
- checklist clarity
- fast turnaround
- inspection-ready result
- optional carpet / window / oven bundle

### strata 页面
要更像：
- common area maintenance
- complaint reduction
- building presentation
- recurring schedule
- manager-friendly reporting / accountability
