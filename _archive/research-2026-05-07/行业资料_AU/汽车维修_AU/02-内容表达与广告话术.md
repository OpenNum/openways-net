## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕被坑，怕乱报价
可用话术：
- Clear upfront quotes before any work begins.
- Honest advice, transparent pricing, no surprise add-ons.
- We explain what’s urgent, what can wait, and why.

### 痛点 2：不懂车，怕沟通困难
可用话术：
- We explain your options in plain language.
- Friendly mechanics who help you understand the problem before you decide.
- Clear service reports and practical recommendations.

### 痛点 3：怕耽误用车
可用话术：
- Fast turnarounds and same-day bookings where available.
- Convenient bookings near home, work, or with mobile service options.
- Keep your car moving with efficient servicing and repairs.

### 痛点 4：怕影响保修
可用话术：
- Logbook servicing completed to manufacturer specifications.
- Protect your new car warranty without paying dealership prices.
- Qualified mechanics using quality parts and approved procedures.

### 痛点 5：怕 pink slip / 检查不过
可用话术：
- Pink slip inspections with practical guidance on common fail items.
- Safety checks that help you understand what needs fixing before rego renewal.
- On-site inspections and follow-up repairs available where needed.

## 7. 中文化表达建议（面向华人市场时可加）
- 悉尼汽车保养与维修
- 粉单检查 / pink slip / rego 前检查
- 中文沟通，先报价再施工
- 不懂车也能听明白，维修优先级讲清楚
- 适合新移民、留学生、家庭用车、通勤车
- 可做保养、刹车、电瓶、故障灯检测、上门服务
- 保修期内也可做 logbook service

## 8. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 绝对化承诺“全悉尼最低价”
- 没依据就写“100% 一次修好所有问题”
- 混淆 pink slip、roadworthy、rego 等不同地区概念
- 用太多技术术语却不解释用户实际收益
- 把 mechanic、smash repair、towing 全写成同一服务而不区分

## 9. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果要做 5 个页面参考文件，文案最好保持：
- 一页一个主场景
- 每页把“适合谁、常见问题、怎么预约、为什么信任你”讲清楚
- pink slip 用 NSW 本地化词汇
- logbook service 必须把“保修不受影响”讲清楚
- 华语市场内容要多解释少堆术语
