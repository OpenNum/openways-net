## 3. 用户痛点对应话术

### 痛点 1：怕乱收费
可用话术：
- Clear upfront pricing with no hidden fees.
- Transparent hourly rates for local moves and fixed quotes for interstate jobs.
- Know what’s included before moving day.

### 痛点 2：怕物品损坏
可用话术：
- Careful, experienced movers who treat your furniture like their own.
- Professional equipment, protective blankets and secure loading for peace of mind.
- From fragile items to bulky furniture, we move with care.

### 痛点 3：怕当天不准时、拖时间
可用话术：
- Reliable Sydney movers who show up on time and work efficiently.
- Fast, organised moving teams to help keep your day on track.
- Ideal for time-sensitive apartment moves, office relocations and key handovers.

### 痛点 4：怕不会打包拆装
可用话术：
- Add packing, unpacking, dismantling and reassembly for a smoother move.
- Need boxes, wrapping or help with bulky furniture? We’ve got it covered.
- One team for moving, packing and storage, without the hassle of coordinating multiple providers.

### 痛点 5：怕沟通不顺
可用话术：
- Friendly support from quote to delivery.
- Clear communication before, during and after the move.
- Chinese-speaking support available for selected Sydney moves.

## 8. 常见中英对照表达
- 搬家服务 = moving services / removals
- 搬运公司 = removalist company
- 搬运工 = movers / removalists
- 本地搬家 = local removals
- 州际搬家 = interstate removals
- 小件搬运 = small removals
- 面包车搬运 = man with a van / van removals
- 打包拆包 = packing and unpacking
- 家具拆装 = dismantling and reassembly
- 仓储 = storage solutions
- 报价 = quote / estimate
- 无隐藏收费 = no hidden fees
- 固定报价 = fixed quote
- 按小时收费 = hourly rates

## 9. 不建议直接照搬的表达
- 没有依据就写“Sydney #1”
- 过度承诺“绝对最低价”
- 对跨州时效做不现实保证
- 把搬家、物流、国际货运、垃圾清运混成一个模糊大词
- 只谈便宜，不谈保护、准时和流程

## 10. 对后续内容生成的提醒
后续如果要做 5 个页面参考文件，文案最好保持：
- 一页一个主场景
- 把“适合谁、怎么收费、包含什么、为什么选你、怎么预约”讲清楚
- 本地搬家和州际搬家分开写
- 办公搬迁要更强调 planning 和 minimal disruption
- 华人页可以加中文沟通，但整体语气仍要像澳洲本地服务站
