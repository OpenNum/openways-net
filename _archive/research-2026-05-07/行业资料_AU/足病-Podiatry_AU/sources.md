## 已参考页面
1. APMA, What is a Podiatrist?
   - https://www.apma.org/patients-and-the-public/what-is-a-podiatrist/
   - 用途：美国市场中 podiatrist 的角色、训练、运动医学/糖尿病足/ wound care 等方向。

2. Mayo Clinic, Plantar fasciitis - Symptoms and causes
   - https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/plantar-fasciitis/symptoms-causes/syc-20354846
   - 用途：heel pain / plantar fasciitis 作为高频需求入口的典型说明。

3. CDC, Your Feet and Diabetes
   - https://www.cdc.gov/diabetes/diabetes-complications/diabetes-and-your-feet.html
   - 用途：糖尿病足风险、神经病变、每日检查与早期处理必要性。
